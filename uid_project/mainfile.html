Features of the Canteen Booking System:

  • Pre-Order Meals - Save time and avoid queues.
  • Live Menu Updates - Check daily specials and meal availability.
  • Digital Payments - Pay using UPI, cards, or student wallets.
  • Order Tracking - Get real-time updates on your order.
  • Special Discounts - Enjoy bulk order offers and meal plans.

How to Use the System?

  1. Log in with your student/faculty credentials.
  2. Select a canteen and browse the menu.
  3. Place your order and choose a pickup time.
  4. Make payment securely online or opt for cash on delivery.
  5. Collect your meal without waiting in long queues!
